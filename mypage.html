Ownership

We don't own your content.

At LyfeLog you would be creating a log of your everyday life, and we know how many sentiments are attached to these experiences. Since this is your life, you retain complete ownership to all your content. All your text posts, videos and photos that you upload to LyfeLog are yours alone. We never claim any rights to your content or ever try to benefit from them since we see that as a violation to your privacy.

 

We also give you the privilege of downloading and importing all your content to your computer, right from the beginning, forming a complete archive of everything you ever shared on LyfeLog. If you wish, you could also delete your account, removing every post you ever made here, since we don't want to chain you down here, but we do persuade you to stay with us.

Connect and Share

Connect your social accounts.

We know you must have a budding network on other social media too and we wouldn't want to restrict your content only in LyfeLog. When you sign up, you have the option to connect your social accounts on Facebook and Twitter and not worry about having your content getting viral only here.

Share your posts.

Once you have connected, you can share all your posts to these social accounts with just one simple tap. When you compose a post you can choose whether you want to share it on Facebook or Twitter, hit send and you're done. By sharing your posts on other accounts, your friends would know how different social networking is at LyfeLog and why they should also join your network and start logging their lives too.
